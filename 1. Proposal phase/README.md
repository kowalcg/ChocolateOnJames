# Chocolat on James - Business Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This comprehensive business analysis project is designed to evaluate and optimize the operations of **Chocolat on James**, an artisan chocolate shop located in the James Street North district of downtown Hamilton, Ontario.

## Business Context

### Current Owner
- **Dan Edwards** - Purchased the business on September 30, 2025 (currently 1 week into ownership)
- Working 12-hour days, 7 days/week
- Minimal business systems in place from previous owner

### Current State Snapshot
- **Annual Revenue**: $537,000 (Jun 2024 - May 2025)
- **Revenue Trend**: Declined from $751K (2021) to $537K (2025) under previous ownership
- **Peak Month**: December ($89K - 2x average)
- **Low Month**: January ($17K - significant post-holiday crash)
- **Seasonality**: 5x variance between peak and low months

### Product Offerings
- Handmade artisan chocolates
- Truffles
- Ice cream
- Coffee
- Premium confections

### Critical Business Challenges
1. **Zero Marketing Infrastructure**
   - No marketing spend historically
   - All revenue from organic walk-in traffic
   - No customer database or email list
   - No digital presence beyond basic website

2. **No E-commerce Platform**
   - Missing online sales channel
   - Unable to capture customers outside local area
   - No shipping/fulfillment capabilities

3. **Systems Gap**
   - No inventory management system
   - No financial tracking system
   - No customer relationship management (CRM)
   - No operational documentation

4. **Severe Seasonality**
   - Revenue volatility creates cash flow challenges
   - 5x variance between peak (December) and low (January) months
   - Need for revenue smoothing strategies

5. **Owner Burnout Risk**
   - 84+ hour work weeks unsustainable
   - Need for operational efficiency improvements
   - Requires systems to reduce owner dependence
