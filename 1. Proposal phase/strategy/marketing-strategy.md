# Marketing Strategy - Chocolat on James

## Executive Summary

This marketing strategy transforms Chocolat on James from zero marketing spend to a data-driven, multi-channel marketing operation. The goal is to drive brand awareness, customer acquisition, and revenue growth through strategic investment in proven marketing channels.

**Current State**: $0 marketing spend, 100% organic walk-in traffic  
**Year 1 Budget**: $70,000 (10.4% of target $675K revenue)  
**Year 1 Goal**: Acquire 3,000+ customers, build 2,500+ email list, drive $100K+ incremental revenue

### 3. Email Marketing ($3,000 platform + management)

**Strategy**: Most cost-effective channel (15-20x ROI typical)

**Email List Building**:
**Goal**: 2,500 subscribers Year 1

**Methods**:
- Website popup: "Get 10% off your first order"
- In-store signup: Tablet at checkout, incentive (10% off next visit)
- Event signup: Art Crawl, tastings
- Online checkout: Opt-in (pre-checked)
- Referrals: Encourage sharing

**Growth Target**: 100-150 new subscribers per month

**Email Flows** (Automated):

1. **Welcome Series** (5 emails over 3 weeks)
   - Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + 10% discount code
   - Email 2 (Day 3): Our story + bestsellers
   - Email 3 (Day 7): How to order + shipping info
   - Email 4 (Day 14): Customer testimonials
   - Email 5 (Day 21): "Don't forget your discount!"

2. **Abandoned Cart** (3 emails)
   - Email 1 (1 hour): "You left something sweet behind"
   - Email 2 (24 hours): Reminder with product images + reviews
   - Email 3 (72 hours): Last chance + 10% discount

3. **Post-Purchase** (3 emails)
   - Email 1 (Immediately): Order confirmation + tracking
   - Email 2 (Delivery + 1 day): "How was your chocolate?" + care tips
   - Email 3 (7 days): Review request + refer-a-friend

4. **Win-Back** (2 emails for 60+ day inactives)
   - Email 1: "We miss you" + new products
   - Email 2 (7 days later): Special comeback offer (15% off)

**Email Campaigns** (Manual, 2-4 per month):
- Weekly or bi-weekly newsletter (Friday mornings)
- New product launches
- Holiday promotions (Valentine's, Mother's Day, Christmas)
- Behind-the-scenes stories
- Exclusive subscriber offers
- Seasonal recipes/pairings
- Art Crawl specials

**Email Performance Targets**:
- Open rate: 25-35%
- Click-through rate: 3-5%
- Conversion rate: 2-4%
- Unsubscribe rate: <0.5%
- Revenue per email: $1-3 per subscriber
- Annual email revenue: $30,000-60,000

**Platform**: Klaviyo ($20-60/month based on list size)

3. **Measure**: Track likes, comments, shares, saves, reach
4. **Learn**: "Reels get 3x more reach but static posts convert better"
5. **Apply**: Adjust content mix based on data

**Areas for Continuous Testing**:
- Ad creative and copy
- Email subject lines and send times
- Landing page layouts
- Offer and promotion types
- Audience targeting
- Budget allocation across channels

**Optimization Cycle**: Monthly reviews with adjustments

## Risk Mitigation

### Marketing Risks

**Risk 1: Low ROI / Wasted Spend**
- **Mitigation**: Start small, test, scale what works; track religiously; cut underperformers fast
- **Early Warning**: ROAS <2x after 3 months

**Risk 2: Ad Account Issues (Facebook/Google suspension)**
- **Mitigation**: Follow platform policies strictly; have backup accounts; diversify channels
- **Response Plan**: Appeal immediately, shift budget to other channels

**Risk 3: Negative Reviews or PR**
- **Mitigation**: Deliver exceptional product and service; respond professionally to issues
- **Response Plan**: Have crisis communication plan; respond quickly and authentically

**Risk 4: Creative Fatigue**
- **Mitigation**: Refresh creative monthly; test new formats; use user-generated content
- **Early Warning**: CTR drops >30% month-over-month

**Risk 5: Budget Overrun**
- **Mitigation**: Set strict monthly budgets; automated spending limits; weekly monitoring
- **Response**: Pause underperforming campaigns; reallocate to winners
