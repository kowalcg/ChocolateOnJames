# E-commerce Strategy - Chocolat on James

## Executive Summary

This e-commerce strategy outlines the path to launching and scaling an online sales channel for Chocolat on James, targeting $100K in Year 1 revenue and $280K+ by Year 2. E-commerce represents the single highest-impact growth opportunity, enabling 24/7 sales, geographic expansion, and customer database development.

**Current State**: No e-commerce capability  
**Year 1 Target**: $100,000 online revenue (15% of total revenue)  
**Timeline**: Launch within 90 days  
**Investment**: $15,000 setup + $20,000 Year 1 marketing

---

## Strategic Rationale

### Why E-commerce is Critical

**1. Revenue Growth**
- Adds new revenue channel without cannibalizing walk-in sales
- Captures customers outside physical location range
- Enables 24/7 selling (not limited to store hours)
- Projected $100K Year 1, $280K Year 2

**2. Market Expansion**
- Hamilton residents who prefer online shopping
- Greater Toronto Area (GTA) market access
- Niagara region accessibility
- National potential (lower priority initially)

**3. Customer Data Collection**
- Every online sale builds customer database
- Email addresses for marketing
- Purchase history for personalization
- Behavior data for optimization

**4. Competitive Parity**
- All major chocolate competitors have e-commerce
- Table stakes for premium chocolate retail
- Risk of losing customers to online competitors

**5. Reduced Seasonality**
- Online sales less seasonal than walk-in
- Marketing can drive sales in slow months
- Corporate gifting easier to manage online
- Subscriptions add recurring revenue

**6. Enhanced Customer Experience**
- Convenience of home delivery
- Easy reordering of favorites
- Browse full selection without visiting store
- Gift shipping to recipients

### Product Descriptions

**Formula for High-Converting Descriptions**:
1. **Headline**: Evocative name
2. **One-line summary**: What it is
3. **Sensory description**: Taste, texture, experience
4. **Ingredients**: Transparency
5. **Occasion suggestions**: When to buy/gift
6. **Size/quantity**: Clear specification
7. **Storage instructions**: Care information

**Example**:
```
Dark Sea Salt Caramel Truffle

Silky dark chocolate ganache with a hint of sea salt, enrobed in 70% dark chocolate.

Bite into the smooth, glossy shell to discover a luscious caramel center that melts on your tongue. The perfect balance of rich cocoa, buttery caramel, and a delicate crunch of fleur de sel creates an unforgettable taste experience.

Ingredients: Dark chocolate (cocoa beans, sugar, cocoa butter), cream, butter, cane sugar, sea salt, vanilla.

Perfect for: The discerning chocolate lover, after-dinner treats, sophisticated gifts, or savoring with a bold red wine.

Each truffle is handcrafted in small batches in Hamilton, Ontario. Approximately 1.25 inches in diameter.

Storage: Best enjoyed within 2 weeks. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Refrigerate only if necessary.
```

**SEO Optimization**:
- Include keywords naturally (artisan, handmade, Hamilton, truffle)
- Clear structure with headers
- 150-300 words per product
- Unique descriptions (never duplicate)

## Shipping & Fulfillment Strategy

### Shipping Options

**Option 1: Local Delivery (Hamilton)**
- Free delivery on orders $75+
- $7.99 delivery under $75
- Same-day delivery (orders by 2pm)
- Next-day delivery standard
- Target 80% of Year 1 online orders

**Option 2: Regional Shipping (Ontario)**
- Courier (UPS, Purolator, Canada Post)
- 1-3 day delivery
- Cold packs included May-September
- Shipping cost calculator at checkout
- Target 15% of Year 1 orders

**Option 3: National Shipping (Canada)**
- Express shipping recommended (2-3 days)
- Available year-round with cold packs
- Phase 2 (after local validation)
- Target 5% of Year 1 orders

### Shipping Costs

**Customer-Facing Rates**:
- Local Hamilton: Free over $75, otherwise $7.99
- Ontario: $12.99-19.99 depending on weight/distance
- Canada-wide: $19.99-29.99

**Actual Costs** (approximate):
- Local delivery (own delivery): $3-5 per order (gas, time)
- Ontario courier: $10-15
- Canada express: $18-25
- Cold packs: $1.50-2.50 each
- Packaging: $2-4 per order

**Margin Impact**:
- Local delivery most profitable
- Free shipping threshold covers cost
- Ontario/Canada shipping margin neutral or small loss
- Make up margin on product sales

### Packaging

**Requirements**:
- Protect chocolates during shipping
- Maintain temperature (cold packs in summer)
- Branded presentation
- Eco-friendly where possible
- Unboxing experience

**Packaging Components**:
1. Outer shipping box (corrugated, branded sticker)
2. Interior chocolate box (premium presentation)
3. Tissue paper or fill
4. Cold pack (seasonal)
5. Branded thank-you card
6. Care/storage instructions card

**Cost**: $3-6 per order

**Supplier**: Uline, local packaging company, or custom (Premium Canada)

### Fulfillment Process

**Order Flow**:
1. Order received (email notification to Dan)
2. Order review (check inventory, special requests)
3. Pick products from inventory
4. Pack with care (quality check)
5. Add cold pack if needed (May-Sept)
6. Generate shipping label
7. Arrange pickup or deliver locally
8. Update order status (tracking email to customer)
9. Request review 5-7 days after delivery

**Labor Time**: 10-20 minutes per order
**Daily Capacity**: 20-30 orders (current staffing)

**Fulfillment Schedule**:
- Orders by 2pm: Same-day processing
- After 2pm: Next-day processing
- Local delivery: Daily (except Sunday)
- Courier pickup: Daily

### Inventory Management

**Integration**: Shopify POS + online inventory sync

**Approach**:
- Real-time inventory tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Reserve stock for walk-in customers (20%)
- Production planning based on online demand
- Seasonal ramp-up for holidays

**Safety Stock**: 1-2 weeks for popular items

## Customer Experience & Conversion Optimization

### Email Capture Strategy

**Goal**: Build email list from day 1

**Methods**:
1. **Popup** (timed or exit-intent): "Get 10% off your first order"
2. **Footer signup**: "Join our chocolate club for exclusive offers"
3. **Checkout**: Opt-in for marketing emails (pre-checked)
4. **Post-purchase**: "Want to hear about new flavors?"

**Incentive**: 10-15% off first purchase, or free shipping

**Target**: 8-10% of visitors provide email

### Cart Abandonment Recovery

**Statistics**: 60-75% of carts are abandoned

**Recovery Strategy**:
1. **1 hour later**: Automated email "You left something sweet behind"
2. **24 hours later**: Reminder email with product images
3. **3 days later**: Last chance email with 10% discount code

**Expected Recovery**: 15-25% of abandoned carts

**Revenue Impact**: $10-20K additional Year 1

**Target**: 15-25% review rate
