## Risks & Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Marketing ROI Below Expectations
**Mitigation**: 
- Test small, scale winners
- Cut underperformers quickly
- Focus on email (highest ROI guaranteed)
- Monthly ROI review

### Risk 2: Corporate Program Slow Uptake
**Mitigation**:
- Start outreach early (Month 5)
- Focus on Q4 booking in Sept-Oct
- Sample aggressively to qualified prospects
- Personal relationship building (Dan's role)
- Lower minimums to reduce barrier

### Risk 3: Subscription Low Adoption
**Mitigation**:
- Strong launch incentive
- Email list promotion (warm audience)
- Easy pause/cancel (reduce friction)
- Excellent product selection
- Can postpone to Year 2 if needed

### Risk 4: Holiday Fulfillment Challenges
**Mitigation**:
- Hire seasonal temp help
- Order supplies well in advance
- Set order cutoffs and communicate clearly
- Over-prepare for peak
- Dan focus on quality control

### Risk 5: Owner Burnout
**Mitigation**:
- Marketing coordinator handles social, email, admin
- Production assistant handles making and fulfillment
- Dan delegates ruthlessly
- Schedule time off (even during busy season)
- Protect Dan's energy as key business asset

### Risk 6: Cash Flow Strain
**Mitigation**:
- $25K line of credit as buffer
- Monitor cash weekly
- Phase investments (don't spend all at once)
- Collect corporate payments promptly
- Maintain 2-3 months operating reserve
