# Growth Opportunities - Chocolat on James

## Overview

This document outlines specific growth opportunities for Chocolat on James, prioritized by impact, feasibility, and timeline. Each opportunity is analyzed for revenue potential, investment requirements, and strategic fit.

---

## Opportunity Evaluation Framework

### Prioritization Criteria

Opportunities are evaluated across four dimensions:

**1. Revenue Impact**
- High: $50K+ Year 1
- Medium: $20-50K Year 1  
- Low: $10-20K Year 1

**2. Investment Required**
- Low: <$5K
- Medium: $5-15K
- High: $15K+

**3. Implementation Difficulty**
- Easy: 1-2 months, low complexity
- Moderate: 2-4 months, moderate complexity
- Complex: 4+ months, high complexity

**4. Strategic Value**
- Core: Essential for competitive parity
- Important: Significant advantage
- Nice-to-have: Incremental benefit

## Opportunity Risks & Considerations

### Common Risk Factors

**1. Owner Capacity Constraint**
- Dan already at 84 hours/week
- Cannot execute all opportunities simultaneously
- **Mitigation**: Prioritize ruthlessly, hire support staff

**2. Investment Capital**
- Requires $60-125K Year 1 investment
- **Mitigation**: Phase investments, start with highest ROI

**3. Execution Quality**
- Poor execution worse than no execution
- **Mitigation**: Focus on fewer opportunities, execute excellently

**4. Marketing ROI Uncertainty**
- Not all marketing delivers expected returns
- **Mitigation**: Start small, test, scale winners, cut losers

**5. Competitive Response**
- Success may attract competitive attention
- **Mitigation**: Move fast, build relationships, own local positioning
