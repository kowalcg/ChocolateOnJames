## Funding Options & Strategies

### Option A: Business Cash Flow + Owner Investment

**Structure**:
- Business generates ~$100-120K cash Year 1
- Dan invests $25-50K from personal savings
- Total: $125-170K available

**Pros**:
- No debt
- No outside investors
- Full control maintained

**Cons**:
- May limit to Essential or Recommended package
- Ties up Dan's personal capital
- No financial buffer

**Best For**: Essential or Recommended package

---

### Option B: Cash Flow + Small Business Loan

**Structure**:
- Business cash flow: $100K
- Dan investment: $25K
- Small business loan or line of credit: $50K
- Total: $175K available

**Loan Terms** (estimated):
- Amount: $50,000
- Term: 5 years
- Rate: 7-10% (current small business rates)
- Monthly payment: ~$990-1,060
- Total interest: ~$9,400-13,600

**Pros**:
- Enables Recommended package comfortably
- Preserves some cash buffer
- Payback from revenue growth
- Interest tax-deductible

**Cons**:
- Monthly debt service ($1,000)
- Qualification requirements
- Personal guarantee likely required

**Best For**: Recommended package with buffer

---

### Option C: Cash Flow + Line of Credit

**Structure**:
- Business cash flow: $100K (covers most expenses)
- Line of credit: $50K (draw as needed)
- Interest only on amounts used

**Terms** (estimated):
- Limit: $50,000
- Rate: Prime + 2-4% (~9-11%)
- Draw as needed
- Pay back as cash flow allows

**Pros**:
- Flexibility to draw only what's needed
- Only pay interest on used amount
- Can pay down early without penalty
- Good for cash flow smoothing (seasonality)

**Cons**:
- Variable rate risk
- Discipline required to not over-draw
- Still requires qualification

**Best For**: Recommended package with maximum flexibility

---

### Option D: Outside Investor

**Structure**:
- Silent partner invests $50-125K
- Equity stake or profit share
- Terms negotiated

**Pros**:
- No debt service
- Potentially brings expertise
- Risk sharing

**Cons**:
- Give up ownership/control
- Profit sharing ongoing
- May complicate decision-making
- Complex to structure fairly

**Best For**: If can't secure other funding and want Recommended or Accelerated

---

## Recommended Funding Approach

For most situations: **Option B (Cash Flow + Small Business Loan)**

**Rationale**:
- Enables full Recommended package ($125K)
- Leaves $50K cushion from $175K raised
- Monthly loan payment ($1,000) easily covered by revenue growth
- Preserves Dan's personal cash for emergencies
- Loan paid back from growth within 3-4 years

**Implementation**:
1. Month 0: Apply for $50K small business loan or line of credit
2. Use business cash flow for Months 1-6 expenses
3. Draw on loan for Months 7-12 as needed
4. Pay back loan from improved cash flow Years 2-4
