## Root Cause Analysis

**Why Did Revenue Decline 28.5%?**

The decline from $751K to $537K was not due to uncontrollable external factors (market, location, product). The root causes are all internal and controllable:

**Primary Causes**:
1. **Zero Marketing Investment**: No customer acquisition efforts
2. **No E-commerce Adaptation**: Failed to adapt to consumer shift online
3. **No Customer Retention**: Lost past customers with no follow-up
4. **No Innovation**: Stagnant product offering and business model
5. **No Systems Investment**: Informal operations limiting efficiency

**Secondary Causes**:
6. COVID-19 impact on walk-in traffic (temporary, but not recovered)
7. Possible previous owner disengagement or health issues
8. General retail foot traffic challenges (shifted online)

**Key Insight**: All primary causes are fixable through strategic investment and execution. This is good news—the problems are within Dan's control to solve.
