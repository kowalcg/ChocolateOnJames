# Revenue Analysis: Chocolat on James
## Financial Performance Assessment (June 2024 - May 2025)

---

## Executive Summary

**Current Financial Health: CONCERNING BUT RECOVERABLE**

Chocolat on James is experiencing a significant decline from its 2021 peak, driven primarily by zero marketing investment and operational challenges. However, the business maintains positive cash flow and demonstrates strong seasonal demand that can be leveraged for growth.

**Key Metrics:**
- Annual Revenue: **$537,438**
- Net Profit: **$307,197** (57% margin)
- Monthly Average: **$44,787**
- 4-Year Decline: **-28%** from 2021 peak of $751,222
- Marketing Investment: **$0** (critical deficiency)

**Critical Finding:** The business is profitable but deteriorating. Without strategic intervention, the downward trend will continue.

### Break-Even Calculation

**Fixed Costs per Month:** $11,687
- Rent: $6,432
- Staff: $4,000
- Utilities/Other: $1,255

**Variable Costs:** 16.7% of revenue (COGS ratio)

**Break-Even Formula:**
```
Break-Even Revenue = Fixed Costs / (1 - Variable Cost %)
Break-Even Revenue = $11,687 / (1 - 0.167)
Break-Even Revenue = $11,687 / 0.833
Break-Even Revenue = $14,032/month or $168,384/year
```

**Current Performance vs. Break-Even:**
- Current Monthly Average: $44,787
- Break-Even: $14,032

The alternative—maintaining the status quo—will result in continued attrition and eventual business viability concerns.
