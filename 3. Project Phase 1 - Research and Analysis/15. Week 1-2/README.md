- **Market Size:** $2-2.7M Hamilton premium chocolate market (annual)
- **Current Share:** 20-27% market share ($537K) - achieved with NO e-commerce!
- **Growth Potential:** $200-400K additional revenue in Year 1 through e-commerce + corporate channels
- **Target Revenue:** $1M+ by Year 3 (30-40% market share - realistic)

### Competitive Landscape
- **Critical Finding:** Only Hamilton competitor without e-commerce (must address)
- **Brand Opportunity:** "Hamilton's Chocolate" positioning is unclaimed - perfect differentiation
- **Unfair Advantages:** Prime location, 14K Instagram followers, ice cream bar, ArtCrawl participation
