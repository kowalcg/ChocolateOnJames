## Contest & UGC Templates – Agent Brief

- **Objective:** prepare campaign kits for seasonal giveaways, customer photo contests, and ambassador spotlights.
- **Components:** announcement email, social captions (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok), landing page copy, entry form (Google Form/Typeform), winner follow-up template, and legal terms snippet.
- **Guidelines:** outline contest mechanics, eligibility, deadlines, prize details, and moderation workflow. Provide examples for Valentine’s, Holiday, and Corporate Appreciation themes.
- **Assets:** include Canva graphics placeholders (link to templates), hashtag suggestions, and instructions for collecting user-generated content with consent.
- **Handoff:** supply launch checklist, reporting tracker (entries, engagement, conversions), and risk notes (legal compliance, privacy).
