After analyzing 15+ competitors across Hamilton and the GTA, no one owns this positioning. This is Chocolat on James's moment to claim it and become the definitive local chocolate destination.

Why This Matters:

  • First-mover advantage - defining the category
  • Media appeal - local news loves Hamilton business stories
  • Partnership power - McMaster, Ti-Cats, hotels want THE Hamilton chocolate brand
  • Local pride - Hamilton supports Hamilton (786K residents)

  • 7. Claim "Hamilton's Chocolate" Positioning Update social bios, email signatures, packaging with tagline. Make every touchpoint reinforce the positioning. Consistent messaging across all channels.
  • 8. Execute Holiday Marketing Campaign Use 50+ Canva templates provided. Post 5-7x/week with daily Stories. Drive foot traffic and pre-orders for December peak season.

Q1 2026 Priorities (January-March)

  • 9. Launch E-commerce on Pavement (January) Go live with full e-commerce including online ordering, shipping zones (Hamilton, GTA, Ontario), local delivery, corporate portals. Target: $105K-137K Year 1 revenue.

  • 13. Implement On-Page SEO Strategy Title tags, meta descriptions, header structure (Pavement-specific). Build 40+ local citations, launch blog content (1-2 posts/week). Target: #1 Local Pack ranking.
