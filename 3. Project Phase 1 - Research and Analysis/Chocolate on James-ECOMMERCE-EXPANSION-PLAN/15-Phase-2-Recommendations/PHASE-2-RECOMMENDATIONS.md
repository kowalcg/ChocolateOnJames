## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**1. E-commerce Launch Delays**
- Risk: Missed Q1 2026 launch
- Mitigation: Begin setup Q4 2025, buffer time, clear timeline
- Status: Platform decision Q4 2025

**2. Technical Issues**
- Risk: Platform bugs or integration problems
- Mitigation: Soft launch testing, backup plans, support resources
- Status: Testing phase included in timeline

**3. Low Initial Traffic**
- Risk: Slow start after launch
- Mitigation: Email list building, social media promotion, paid advertising
- Status: Marketing budget allocated

**4. Inventory Management**
- Risk: Stockouts or overstock
- Mitigation: Inventory forecasting, reorder points, safety stock
- Status: Inventory management system setup
