- **2021 Peak:** $751,222

- Economy and chocolate price increases compounded the issue

**Seasonality:**
- December: 2-3x higher than normal months
- Extreme variance creates cash flow challenges

### 12. E-COMMERCE STRATEGY DISCUSSION

**Dan's Vision for Online Store:**
- Customers view available products online
- Order for in-store pickup initially
- Add delivery capability quickly after
- **Box Builder Feature:** Custom corporate/gift baskets
  - Select specific items
  - Choose basket size
  - See real-time pricing
  - Essential for corporate orders

**Target Markets for Online:**
- **New condo buildings:** 15 buildings, 500-600 people each, within 2km
  - Younger crowds
  - Heavy online ordering/delivery users
- **McMaster University:** 40,000 students
  - Impulsive buyers with some disposable income
  - Designated pickup times/locations on campus (e.g., "Thode Library 7-8 PM")

**Timing Considerations:**
- Concerned about launching before Christmas due to:
  - Stock management challenges
  - 2-3x increased demand
  - Learning curve with new systems
- **Alternative approach:** Launch with limited selection
  - Easily available items: Panned chocolates, truffles
  - Test systems before full rollout
- **Realistic timeline:** New Year launch (January-February)

**Greg's Technical Note:** 
"Pickup and delivery options are just a switch - straightforward technical implementation"
