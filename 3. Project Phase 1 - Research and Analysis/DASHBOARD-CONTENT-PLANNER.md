## How to Use This Template

1. **BEFORE the meeting:** Fill out as much as you can from your work
2. **DURING the meeting:** Take notes and update in real-time
3. **AFTER the meeting:** Finalize all sections
4. **THEN:** Copy/paste into your HTML dashboard template

**Time saved:** Instead of searching through the HTML wondering what to put where, you have everything organized and ready to paste!
