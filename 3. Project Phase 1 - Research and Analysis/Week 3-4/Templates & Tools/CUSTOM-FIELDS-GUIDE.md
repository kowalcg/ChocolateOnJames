## üìû Questions?

**Common Questions:**

**Q: Can I use all three custom fields at once?**
A: YES! This creates the most targeted content possible.

**Q: Do I have to fill in custom fields?**
A: No. Only use them when you need that specific flexibility. Standard dropdowns work great for most content!

**Q: Can I save my custom field combinations?**
A: The tool doesn't save them YET, but we recommend keeping a document with your favorite combinations for easy reuse.

**Q: What if my custom text is too long?**
A: Keep custom fields concise (2-5 words). Example: "Partnership with Coffee Shop" not "Partnership with the amazing coffee shop down the street that we love"

**Q: Will this work with all AI platforms?**
A: Yes! Custom fields enhance prompts for ALL 8 AI platforms.
