## Using the Generated Prompts

### With ChatGPT (DALL-E 3) - RECOMMENDED FOR BEGINNERS

**ChatGPT Plus Required** ($20/month)

1. Copy the generated prompt
2. Open ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)
3. Paste the prompt and say: "Generate an image with this description"
4. ChatGPT will create 1-2 images
5. Ask for variations: "Create 3 more variations"
6. Download your favorites

**Pros:**
- Easiest to use
- Great at following detailed instructions
- Includes text naturally
- High quality results

**Cons:**
- Requires ChatGPT Plus subscription
- Limited to 50 images per 3 hours

---

### With Midjourney - BEST FOR ARTISTIC IMAGES

**Subscription Required** ($10-$60/month)

1. Join Midjourney Discord (midjourney.com)
2. Go to any #general channel
3. Type `/imagine` and paste your prompt
4. Wait 60 seconds for results
5. Upscale favorites with U1, U2, U3, U4 buttons
6. Create variations with V1, V2, V3, V4 buttons

**Midjourney Tips:**
- Add `--ar 1:1` for square images
- Add `--ar 9:16` for stories
- Add `--ar 16:9` for landscape
- Add `--v 6` for latest version
- Add `--style raw` for photorealistic

**Pros:**
- Stunning artistic quality
- Fast generation
- Unlimited variations

**Cons:**
- Steeper learning curve
- Discord-based (can be confusing)
- Less good at text

---

### With Adobe Firefly - BEST FOR COMMERCIAL USE

**Free Tier Available** (25 credits/month)

1. Go to firefly.adobe.com
2. Select "Text to Image"
3. Paste your prompt
4. Adjust style settings
5. Generate (uses 1 credit)
6. Download

**Pros:**
- Commercially safe (no copyright issues)
- Easy to use
- Free tier available

**Cons:**
- Limited free generations
- Less creative than Midjourney

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Image doesn't match brand colors
**Solution:** Regenerate and specifically mention "ensure colors match brand palette: #3E2723, #F5F5DC, #D4AF37"

### Problem: Too much text in image
**Solution:** Add to prompt: "minimal or no text, leave space for text overlay"

### Problem: Doesn't look professional
**Solution:** Add: "professional commercial photography, high-end studio quality, magazine-worthy"

### Problem: Wrong composition
**Solution:** Specify: "centered composition" or "rule of thirds" or "negative space on left for text"

### Problem: Chocolate looks artificial
**Solution:** Add: "photorealistic, authentic chocolate texture, professional food photography"

## FAQs

**Q: Can I use AI-generated images commercially?**
A: Yes! ChatGPT (DALL-E 3) and Adobe Firefly images are commercially licensed. Midjourney requires paid subscription for commercial use.

**Q: Will my images look AI-generated?**
A: With good prompts, AI images are indistinguishable from professional photography.

**Q: How many variations should I generate?**
A: Generate 5-10 variations, pick the best 1-2.

**Q: Can I edit AI images?**
A: Yes! Edit in Photoshop, Canva, or any image editor.

**Q: What if the image isn't perfect?**
A: Regenerate with modified prompt, or edit manually.

**Q: Can I use real product photos instead?**
A: Yes! Use AI for concept art, use real photos for actual products when available.
