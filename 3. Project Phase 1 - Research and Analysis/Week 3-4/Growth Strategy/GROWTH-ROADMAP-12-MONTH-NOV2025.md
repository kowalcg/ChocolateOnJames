## Executive Summary

This 12-month roadmap transforms Chocolat on James from a single-channel walk-in business ($537K annual revenue) to a multi-channel chocolate authority targeting $698K-806K in Year 1. The strategy prioritizes quick wins in Q4 2025, launches e-commerce in Q1 2026, and scales through corporate gifting, partnerships, and brand authority.
