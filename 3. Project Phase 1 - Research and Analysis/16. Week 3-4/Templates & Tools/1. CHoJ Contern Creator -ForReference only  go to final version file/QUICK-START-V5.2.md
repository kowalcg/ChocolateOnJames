## The Two Workflows

### Workflow A: Quick Variations (Original v5.1 Behavior)
Perfect for: Getting instant variations of an inspiration image

1. Upload your chocolate image (drag, drop, paste, or click)
2. Wait 2 seconds for AI analysis
3. Review 4 prompt variations:
   - Exact Match
   - Inspired Variation
   - Premium Elevated
   - CHoJ Branded
4. Click "Copy" or "Use" on your favorite
5. Done!

**Time:** 30 seconds

---

### Workflow B: Full Customization (NEW v5.2 Feature!)
Perfect for: Using your product photo with seasonal/audience targeting

1. Upload your chocolate image (same as above)

3. Look for green banner above Generate button:

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I use the same image for multiple posts?
**A:** Yes! Upload once, then:
1. Generate for Instagram + Valentine's
2. Change season to Christmas, Generate again
3. Change audience to Corporate, Generate again
4. All use the same uploaded image!

### Q: What if I want to start fresh?
**A:** Click "Clear Image" button in the green banner, or click "Clear All Fields & Start Fresh" at bottom

### Q: Can I still use quick variations?
**A:** Absolutely! Click "Use" on any of the 4 variations to skip the left panel controls

### Q: What if I don't upload an image?
**A:** Tool works exactly like v5.1 - generates generic chocolate images based on your settings

### Q: Which is better - quick variations or full customization?
**A:**
- **Quick Variations:** Fast, great for inspiration, 4 different styles
- **Full Customization:** More control, seasonal targeting, audience-specific, platform-optimized

## Troubleshooting

### Banner won't show
- Make sure image upload completed (see preview)
- Refresh page and try again
- Check file is JPG, PNG, or WebP

### Generated prompt doesn't use my image
- Verify green banner is visible
- Check button says "...Using Uploaded Image"
- Make sure you didn't click "Use" on quick variation

### Want generic prompts again
- Click "Clear Image" button
- OR click "Clear All Fields & Start Fresh"

## Best Practices

1. Upload high-quality product photos
2. Use landscape or square images (easier to analyze)
3. Good lighting in uploaded image = better results
4. Clear subject (chocolate as focus)
5. Natural backgrounds work best
