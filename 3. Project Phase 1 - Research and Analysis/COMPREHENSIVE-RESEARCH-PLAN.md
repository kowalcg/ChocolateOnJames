## Research Methodology Framework

### 1. **Primary Research (Direct Data Collection)**

#### A. Customer Research
**Methodology:** Mixed qualitative and quantitative
- **In-Depth Interviews:** 20-30 current customers
- **Focus Groups:** 3-4 groups of 8-10 participants each
- **Customer Surveys:** 200+ responses
- **Observational Studies:** Store visits and customer behavior analysis

**Key Questions:**
- What drives chocolate purchasing decisions?
- How do customers discover new chocolate brands?
- What are the pain points in current experience?
- What would increase purchase frequency?
- How much are customers willing to pay for premium chocolate?

#### B. Market Research
**Methodology:** Comprehensive market analysis
- **Demographic Analysis:** Hamilton and surrounding areas
- **Psychographic Profiling:** Lifestyle and behavior patterns
- **Market Size Estimation:** Total addressable market
- **Growth Projections:** 3-5 year market trends
- **Seasonal Analysis:** Year-round purchasing patterns

#### C. Competitive Intelligence
**Methodology:** Deep competitive analysis
- **Direct Competitors:** 15+ local and regional competitors
- **Indirect Competitors:** Gift shops, grocery stores, online retailers
- **Pricing Analysis:** Complete pricing comparison across all products
- **Marketing Analysis:** Advertising, social media, promotional strategies
- **Customer Reviews:** Analysis of competitor customer feedback
