## Executive Summary

This technical SEO audit reveals significant opportunities for improvement in the current chocolatonjames.com website. The site has basic functionality but lacks modern e-commerce capabilities, SEO optimization, and mobile responsiveness. Key findings include outdated content, limited product information, and no online ordering functionality.

### Critical Issues Identified:
- **Outdated Content:** References to previous owner (Sheryl Cronsbury)
- **No E-commerce:** No online ordering or product catalog
- **Limited SEO:** Basic meta tags, no structured data
- **Mobile Issues:** Potential mobile responsiveness problems
- **Content Gaps:** Minimal product information and descriptions
